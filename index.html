NPA Banking Analysis

Did IBC 2016 Actually Work?

Statistical analysis of Non-Performing Assets across PSU, Private, and Foreign banks (2012–2024). Examines whether the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code had measurable impact on NPA recovery.

India Tourism Recovery

Post-COVID State Analysis

State-wise analysis of foreign vs domestic tourist arrivals and recovery patterns post-COVID (2015–2023). Identifies which states recovered fastest and whether recovery was foreign or domestically driven.

BigQuery SQL Tableau Window Functions

Key Finding: 3 states (Tamil Nadu, Uttar Pradesh, Delhi) account for 60% of foreign tourist arrivals.

Early Transaction Patterns

Cohort Analysis of 1M Bank Transactions

Cohort-based analysis of customer engagement decay across 1M bank transactions. Identifies which transaction patterns predict long-term customer retention vs churn, and how early engagement metrics correlate with lifetime customer value.

R BigQuery SQL Tableau Cohort Analysis

Key Finding: Customers with 3+ transaction types in first 30 days show 3x higher retention vs single-transaction cohorts.
