Did India's Tourism Recover Equally After COVID?

Tourism Recovery Analysis

State-level analysis of India's domestic and international tourism patterns from 2019–2022. Examines recovery trajectories, regional disparities, and structural shifts in travel behaviour following the pandemic — with implications for state revenue and marketing strategy.

Key Finding

Domestic tourism fully recovered by 2021 — foreign tourism still 40% below 2019 as of 2022. Lakshadweep and small UTs surged 300%+ due to pent-up island demand; major metros rebounded fastest due to international connectivity.

03

Do Early Transaction Patterns Predict Loan Default?

Berka Bank Cohort Analysis

Cohort analysis of 1M+ transactions from a Czech bank (1993–1998). Uses BigQuery for feature engineering, logistic regression in R to model default risk, and Tableau for dashboard delivery. Identifies which early account behaviours signal future credit risk.

Key Finding

Low-frequency accounts (1–4 transactions in first 90 days) default at 25% vs 9.6% for moderate-frequency accounts. Each additional active month in the first 3 months reduces default odds by 20%.
